Dot AI Platform

Dot AI is a multi-model AI platform developed by Prospello Technologies with DotcoSpace to automate and optimize business operations.

  • Built as an agentic AI system that combines specialized models to manage real-world workflows across industries.
  • Automates workflows, manages operations, and reduces manual dependency across a business.
  • Designed to make AI practical, accessible, and results-driven for real business use.

Dot AI V2

Dot AI V2 improves the original platform with stronger contextual understanding, more reliable responses, and broader workflow coverage. Built from real-world usage and feedback, it is designed for business automation, customer interaction, and operational support.

  • Trained on 200,000+ business workflows.
  • Supports 25+ languages, including Malayalam, Hindi, Tamil, Manglish, and English.
  • Continuously evolves to simplify operations, reduce costs, and create scalable business systems.

AI Pre-Sales Agent

An AI-powered pre-sales agent that acts as a dedicated sales assistant. It manages front-end sales workflows, including lead engagement, qualification, real-time conversations, and early-stage customer guidance.

  • Handles lead engagement and initial connection.
  • Qualifies leads using business-specific criteria.
  • Supports real-time conversations with prospects.
  • Can be customized to match business workflow, sales strategy, and brand tone.
